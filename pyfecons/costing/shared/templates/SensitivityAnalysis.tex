\section{Sensitivity Analysis}

A sensitivity analysis was performed on the LCOE calculation by perturbing each of the sensitivity_n_params scalar input parameters by 1\% and measuring the resulting change in LCOE (baseline: \$sensitivity_lcoe_baseline/MWh). The elasticity $\varepsilon_i$ of each parameter $x_i$ is defined as:

\begin{equation}
\varepsilon_i = \frac{\partial(\text{LCOE})}{\partial x_i} \cdot \frac{x_i}{\text{LCOE}}
\end{equation}

An elasticity of $|\varepsilon| = 1$ means a 1\% change in the parameter produces a 1\% change in LCOE. Table~\ref{tab:sensitivity} shows the sensitivity_top_n most influential parameters, ranked by $|\varepsilon|$.
